A Better Way to Handle Doors

Too many door jobs come with vague quotes, missed appointments, and repairs that don't hold. We built BH Door Solutions to be the opposite: clear pricing, on-time service, and workmanship that stands up to Michigan's seasons. When we say we'll fix it right the first time, we mean it.

You deal with real local tradespeople from the first phone call to the final walkthrough. We listen to the problem, explain your options in plain English, and get the work done without upselling you on things you don't need.

Standards We Don't Compromise On

Every door we touch is installed level, sealed tight, and tested before we consider the job done. We show up with fully stocked trucks so most repairs and installs wrap up in a single visit — no waiting days for a part to arrive. We're licensed, insured, and back our labor with a workmanship guarantee.

We know how hard freeze-thaw cycles, road salt, and lake-effect weather are on doors and hardware. So we choose materials and techniques built to handle real Michigan conditions, not just look good on install day.

Proudly Serving Metro Detroit

We're a service-area business, which means we come to you. From Dearborn and Livonia to Troy, Warren, and Royal Oak, we cover Metro Detroit and nearby suburbs within about 35 miles across Wayne, Oakland, and Macomb counties.

There's no storefront to drive to — just a quick call to (313) 236-4558 or a message to schedule your free estimate at a time that works for you.

The Only Door Company You'll Need

Residential or commercial, installation or repair, entry door or patio slider — we handle all of it under one roof. That means you're not juggling multiple contractors or wondering who to call when something goes wrong. One trusted local team, for every door you own.

Whether you're boosting curb appeal, cutting a winter draft, or securing your business, we treat the work like it's on our own home — because our reputation in this community depends on it.
