How we earn every review

Trust is built on the job site, not on a webpage. Before any work starts you get a free on-site assessment and a flat-rate quote in plain English, whether the visit is a quick door repair, a sliding door repair, or a full entry door installation. You approve the price before we pick up a tool.

During the work we protect your floors, keep the site tidy, and test everything before we pack up: the swing, the latch, the lock, and the seal. When we leave, your door closes flush, locks smoothly, and keeps the weather outside where it belongs. If anything is not right afterward, the workmanship guarantee means we come back and make it right.

That is the standard on every job in every suburb we serve, from Warren to Novi. See the kind of work we do in the project gallery, or check whether we cover your city on the service areas page.
