One local crew, three counties

BH Door Solutions is a service-area business: there is no showroom, so you never pay for one. Our trucks run routes through Wayne, Oakland, and Macomb counties every working day, which is how same-day door repair stays realistic across a 35-mile radius whenever the schedule allows.

The suburbs we serve do not all need the same work. Communities near the water like St. Clair Shores see more fogged glass and swollen frames, established inner-ring cities like Warren and Ferndale keep us busy with worn hinges and sliding door repairs, and newer subdivisions in Novi and Canton lean toward entry door upgrades and hardware. Each city page covers the local details, from the services requested most to how scheduling works in your area.
