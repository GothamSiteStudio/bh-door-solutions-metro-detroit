South Lyon holds down Oakland County's southwest corner with a genuine old downtown along Lafayette Street, the landmark Witch's Hat depot beside it, and rings of newer subdivisions built on what used to be farmland. Two kinds of housing means two kinds of door problems, and BH Door Solutions handles both, with free estimates and flat-rate pricing.

Door installation & repair South Lyon homeowners rely on

The blocks around downtown carry some of the oldest homes we work on in this part of the county. A century of settling leaves frames out of square, so original doors drag, gap at the corners, and let drafts through no matter how hard the storm door works. These openings need measurement and patience, not a stock slab forced into place.

The subdivisions off Pontiac Trail and Ten Mile are a different story: builder-grade doors from the 1990s through the 2010s wearing out together. Entries lose their seal, hollow-core interior doors sound like cardboard, and sliders start to drag. We replace what is done, repair what is not, and always tell you which is which.

We cover every South Lyon street, downtown and subdivision alike, along with the neighboring communities listed on our service areas page. One call covers measurement, materials, and installation.

Door replacement in South Lyon, MI

Thinking about door replacement in South Lyon? We'll walk you through material choices, energy efficiency, and real pricing before you commit, and if a repair will honestly do the job, we'll say so. Most South Lyon replacements, including entry doors and storm doors, are finished the same day we start.

Entry and storm doors for every era

A new insulated entry door transforms comfort in an older home, and a quality storm door protects it while adding an easy screen season. We fit both to the real opening, whether it was framed in 1920 or 2010.

Interior doors and honest repairs

We hang and trim interior doors so every room closes cleanly, and our door repair service handles sagging hinges, sticking slabs, and latches that miss the strike, usually in a single visit.

The doors South Lyon homes need most

South Lyon's work splits neatly: careful fitting and repair in the older downtown blocks, and replacements for builder-grade doors in the newer subdivisions. Our trucks arrive stocked, so most repair visits are finished in a single trip, and every job ends with a free estimate honored, honest advice given, and a flat-rate quote kept.

Door service in South Lyon — FAQs

Do you serve all of South Lyon?

Yes. We cover the whole city, from the older blocks around Lafayette Street downtown to the newest subdivisions at the edges, and the surrounding communities too. We are already working across Oakland County most days, so scheduling a visit is usually quick.

How quickly can you come out for a door repair in South Lyon?

Often the same day or the next business day, whenever our schedule allows. For a door that will not lock, close, or latch, call early in the day during our business hours, Sunday through Thursday 9am to 5pm and Friday 9am to noon, and we will give you an honest arrival window.

Can you fix a door in an older downtown home without replacing it?

Often, yes. Many older South Lyon doors just need the frame squared, the hinges reset, and fresh weatherstripping to work like they should. We will tell you honestly whether repair or replacement is the better value before any work starts.
