Huntington Woods calls itself the City of Homes, and it earns the name: a leafy grid of Tudors, colonials, and center-entrance brick houses between Woodward Avenue and the Detroit Zoo, many of them approaching a century old. Doors on houses like these deserve a careful hand. BH Door Solutions installs and restores them with the patience the neighborhood expects, backed by free estimates and flat-rate pricing.

Door installation & repair Huntington Woods homeowners rely on

The housing stock here is unusually consistent: 1920s through 1940s brick homes with arched entries, paneled wood doors, divided-lite sidelites, and original hardware that has outlived several furnaces. Those details are why people move to Huntington Woods, and they are exactly what a careless replacement ruins. Mature trees shade the streets, and shade means damp, which works on wood doors season after season.

Our first instinct on an older door is to save it: realign the slab, re-mortise worn hinges, ease a rubbing edge, and renew the weatherstripping so it seals without slamming. When an owner wants a new door, we source units with the proportions and panel layouts that suit a Tudor arch or a colonial surround, and we hang them plumb in openings that have had ninety years to settle.

We work throughout Huntington Woods, from the streets beside the Detroit Zoo to the blocks near Woodward and the Rackham golf course, and across the rest of southeast Oakland County. See every community we cover on our service areas page.

Door service in Huntington Woods — FAQs

Do you serve all of Huntington Woods?

Yes. The city is small and we cover every street, from the blocks beside the Detroit Zoo to the homes near Woodward Avenue. We are already working across Oakland County most days, so scheduling a visit is usually quick. Call and we will confirm your address.

How quickly can you come out for a door repair in Huntington Woods?

Often the same day or the next business day, whenever our schedule allows. For a door that will not lock, close, or latch, call early in the day during our business hours, Sunday through Thursday 9am to 5pm and Friday 9am to noon, and we will give you an honest arrival window.

Can you match a new door to a Tudor or colonial home?

Yes. We source doors in the proportions, panel layouts, and finishes that suit older architecture, including arched openings and entries with sidelites. The goal is a door that looks original to the house while sealing and locking like a modern unit, backed by our workmanship guarantee.
