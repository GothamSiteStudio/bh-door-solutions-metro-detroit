Door installation & repair Novi homeowners rely on

Novi has grown fast, and it shows in its housing. The subdivisions around Island Lake, Tollgate, and the Twelve Oaks corridor are full of newer colonials and estate homes where a premium fiberglass entry door does real work — setting the tone for the whole facade. We install high-end entry systems with sidelights and transoms that match the scale of these homes, plus the French and modern glass doors that open onto Novi's decks, sunrooms, and walkout basements.

Michigan's freeze-thaw swings are hard on doors, and Novi's mix of open, newer subdivisions means a lot of homes catch the wind coming off the surrounding lakes and farmland. Winter drafts sneak in around worn weatherstripping and settled frames, exterior doors swell and stick through humid summers, and a deep freeze can leave a latch or deadbolt refusing to turn. We handle the adjustments, weatherproofing, and replacements that keep Novi doors sealed tight year-round.

New construction brings its own quirks — as a home settles over its first few years, doors that once hung perfectly can start to drag, gap, or bind. We straighten out those issues fast, and we cover Novi's commercial side too, from the offices and retail spaces along Grand River Avenue and Novi Road to storefronts near Twelve Oaks. Residential or commercial, it's one call and, whenever possible, one visit with a fully stocked truck.

The doors Novi homes need most

For Novi, entry door installation and replacement is where we spend most of our time — these are homes where curb appeal and energy efficiency both matter, so premium fiberglass and steel entry systems, French doors, and large modern glass doors are in high demand.
