Repair or replace? Picking the right door service

A door that binds, drags, or won't latch is usually a repair, not a replacement, and that goes for the frame too. The door frame repair Detroit homeowners ask for after wood rot, storm damage, or a break-in is one of our most common calls, and it costs a fraction of a full entry replacement.

Patio sliders are their own specialty. Worn rollers, bent tracks, and fogged glass panels call for sliding door repair in Detroit and its suburbs, which we handle with parts already stocked on the truck.

Replacement makes sense when a slab is rotted through, a frame has failed structurally, or you want an efficiency and curb-appeal upgrade. In that case, compare entry door installation options and check the cost guide for typical Metro Detroit price ranges. Businesses can go straight to commercial door repair. Not sure which way to go? Request a free estimate and we'll give you an honest answer either way.
